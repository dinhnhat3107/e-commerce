About Powers Sport

About information of Powers Sport


Powers Sport is the leading prestigious shoe store in HUTECH.


The store was formed in 2022, this is a store that specializes in providing specialized sports products from famous brands, providing customers with an easy
shopping experience, helping to improve the user experience.
We believe that online shopping is above our store, accessible, easy and fun. This is the vision
that Powers Sport aspires to and provides to customers every day.
